# Internal Linking Policy

Internal links are part of the asset's governance system.

They should move readers from isolated terms into the full category structure.

## Core Link Rules

Every page should link to:

- the definition page where relevant;
- the clinical boundary page when risk language appears;
- the sources page when medical claims appear;
- ontology or standard pages when classification language appears;
- related measurement pages where helpful.

## Boundary Links

Pages about diabetes, illness, medication, DKA concern, high ketones, or emergency language must link to:

- `/clinical-boundary/`;
- `/dka-concern-boundary/` when applicable;
- `/sources/`.

## Tool Output Links

Signal Map outputs must link to:

- the relevant measurement page;
- the relevant context page;
- the relevant boundary page;
- the source page.

## No Orphan Pages

No route should exist without:

- at least one incoming link from a hub;
- at least three outgoing internal links;
- a clear role in the route map.
