# Clinical Boundary

Ketonemia.com is an educational and reference asset. It is not a clinical service.

## Boundary Statement

The asset may explain blood ketone signals, measurement contexts, and cited guidance. It must not diagnose, prescribe, triage, or replace clinical or emergency care.

## Allowed Language

The asset may say:

- This signal requires context.
- The same ketone number can mean different things in different contexts.
- Some ketone readings, especially in diabetes or illness contexts, may require professional medical attention.
- Cited guidance describes certain ranges as requiring contact with a diabetes care team or urgent care.

## Prohibited Language

The asset must not say:

- You have DKA.
- You do not have DKA.
- You are safe.
- You should treat yourself this way.
- This reading means you need or do not need emergency care.
- This tool can determine your diagnosis.

## High-Risk Topics

Extra caution is required for:

- diabetes;
- DKA;
- euglycemic DKA;
- SGLT2 inhibitor contexts;
- vomiting, dehydration, infection, or serious illness;
- pregnancy;
- pediatric readers;
- emergency symptoms;
- insulin adjustment;
- clinical thresholds.

## Interface Requirement

Any tool output touching K4 or K5 states must include:

- "This is educational information, not a diagnosis."
- a link to the relevant boundary page;
- source references;
- no individualized treatment instruction.
