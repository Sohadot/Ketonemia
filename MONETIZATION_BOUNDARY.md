# Monetization Boundary

Revenue must extend trust, not replace it.

## Permitted Monetization

Ketonemia.com may monetize through:

- premium category briefs;
- continuous ketone monitoring briefs;
- blood ketone measurement landscape reports;
- educational briefs;
- DKA concern language briefs;
- AI reference packs;
- licensed glossary or taxonomy data;
- licensed patient education language;
- embeddable signal map licensing;
- clearly disclosed sponsored reference sections;
- institutional education partnerships;
- qualified strategic inquiries.

## Restricted Monetization

These require explicit review before use:

- directories;
- lead generation;
- sponsored tools;
- device comparisons;
- provider listings;
- product landscape pages.

## Prohibited Monetization

Do not use:

- keto supplement affiliate spam;
- hidden sponsorship;
- fear-based medical lead generation;
- paid rankings presented as neutral;
- diagnosis or triage tools;
- emergency advice funnels;
- content written primarily for affiliate conversion;
- generic ad clutter that damages trust.

## Sponsorship Rule

Use "Supported by" rather than "recommended by" unless a formal endorsement framework exists.
