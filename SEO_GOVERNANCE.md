# SEO Governance

SEO exists to strengthen the asset, not dilute it.

## Allowed SEO Strategy

Use:

- reference-grade pages;
- stable canonical URLs;
- clear definitions;
- source-linked medical explanations;
- audience-specific pages;
- glossary relationships;
- internal linking clusters;
- AI-readable summaries;
- route discipline.

## Prohibited SEO Strategy

Do not create:

- random keyword pages;
- thin pages;
- duplicated explainers;
- unsourced medical claims;
- unsafe symptom-targeted pages;
- keto hype content;
- excessive wellness trend pages;
- keyword pages with no category purpose;
- pages built only because a keyword has volume.

## Page Admission Test

A new route must have:

- a defined audience;
- a category role;
- internal links in and out;
- source requirements;
- boundary requirements if medical;
- a reason it cannot be merged into an existing page.

## Canonical Cluster Logic

Every page should belong to one of these clusters:

- Core definition;
- Standard and ontology;
- Public measurement;
- Clinical boundary;
- Institutional literacy;
- Product and market;
- AI reference;
- Strategic availability.
