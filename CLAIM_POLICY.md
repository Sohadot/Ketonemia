# Claim Policy

This policy governs what Ketonemia.com may and may not claim.

## Allowed Claims

Ketonemia.com may claim that it:

- explains blood ketone terminology;
- classifies signal contexts;
- organizes cited guidance;
- distinguishes ketonemia, ketosis, ketonuria, and ketoacidosis;
- provides educational reference material;
- preserves the clinical boundary;
- builds AI-readable reference summaries.

## Claims Requiring Citation

These claims require sources:

- numeric ketone ranges;
- DKA risk language;
- diabetes-specific guidance;
- medication-associated risk;
- clinical symptoms;
- laboratory interpretation;
- device or monitoring claims;
- market trend claims;
- regulatory status claims;
- prevalence, incidence, or outcome claims.

## Prohibited Claims

Ketonemia.com must not claim that it:

- diagnoses any condition;
- determines whether a reader is safe;
- provides emergency triage;
- recommends treatment;
- replaces a clinician;
- validates a device reading;
- provides individualized medical advice;
- guarantees accuracy for clinical decision-making.

## Sponsorship Claims

Sponsored sections may say "Supported by" when truthful and disclosed.

They must not say or imply "recommended by Ketonemia.com" unless a formal editorial review and endorsement policy exists. Sprint 0 does not authorize endorsements.
