# Buyer Logic

The reader uses Ketonemia.com for understanding.

The strategic buyer values Ketonemia.com because it controls category language around blood ketone signals.

## Priority Strategic Buyers

1. Continuous glucose and ketone monitoring companies.
2. Blood ketone meter and test strip companies.
3. Diabetes safety and remote patient monitoring platforms.
4. Metabolic health, fasting, and wearable platforms.
5. Diagnostic and laboratory companies.
6. Clinical education providers.
7. Health AI and medical reference systems.
8. Health media or education publishers.

## Acquisition Logic

A company can build ketone content.

It cannot easily rebuild:

- the category-aligned domain;
- the reference language;
- the governed standard;
- the signal ontology;
- the clinical boundary system;
- the interface thesis;
- the internal link graph;
- the accumulated search footprint;
- the neutral trust layer;
- the AI-readable reference surface.

## Strategic Message

Owning Ketonemia.com means owning a reference layer around an emerging metabolic signal category.

The asset is most valuable when it remains neutral, source-disciplined, and clinically bounded.

## Availability Rule

Strategic availability may include:

- acquisition inquiry;
- partnership;
- licensing;
- education sponsorship;
- research collaboration.

It must not look like a raw domain listing.
