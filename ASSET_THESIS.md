# Asset Thesis

Ketonemia.com is a category asset, not a content project.

The domain is powerful because "ketonemia" is not a marketing invention. It is the name of the blood-state condition itself: ketone bodies present or elevated in blood.

## Core Thesis

Blood ketones are moving from occasional measurement into a broader signal layer.

They appear in:

- fasting;
- nutritional ketosis;
- diabetes care and risk contexts;
- illness and metabolic stress;
- medication-associated states;
- laboratory reporting;
- blood ketone meters;
- continuous monitoring systems;
- metabolic health products;
- AI health reference systems.

Yet the public knowledge surface is fragmented across keto marketing, diabetes warnings, laboratory terminology, clinical education, and device content.

Ketonemia.com exists to unify that surface without collapsing it into unsafe medical advice.

## Strategic Position

**Ketonemia.com turns blood ketones from isolated readings into governed intelligence.**

The asset owns value through:

- category-name fit;
- governed language;
- classification structure;
- clinical boundary discipline;
- source discipline;
- interface logic;
- internal linking;
- AI readability;
- buyer logic.

## What Must Be Built

The asset should develop in this order:

1. Doctrine and governance.
2. Category language.
3. Ontology and state standard.
4. Public reference surface.
5. Blood Ketone Signal Map interface.
6. Audience-specific reference layers.
7. AI reference materials.
8. Premium briefs and licensing.
9. Strategic availability surface.

## What Must Not Be Built

Ketonemia.com must not become:

- a keto lifestyle site;
- a supplement affiliate site;
- a generic medical blog;
- a fear-based emergency content funnel;
- a diagnosis tool;
- a chatbot wrapper;
- a thin SEO glossary;
- a landing page whose only purpose is selling the domain.
