# Foundation Doctrine

Ketonemia.com operates as **Blood Ketone Intelligence Infrastructure**.

The asset does not sell ketosis. It owns the reference layer for understanding blood ketone signals.

## Governing Belief

**Ketonemia is a blood signal before it is a clinical verdict.**

Ketonemia names the presence or elevation of ketone bodies in blood. That state can appear in different contexts, including fasting, nutritional ketosis, exercise, illness, diabetes-associated risk, medication contexts, laboratory testing, and emergency concern boundaries.

The asset must classify the signal without converting the signal into a diagnosis.

## Operating Rule

**Classify the signal. Preserve the clinical boundary.**

Ketonemia.com may:

- define ketonemia and related terms;
- explain measurement types;
- classify signal contexts;
- cite external guidance;
- identify educational boundaries;
- route readers to relevant reference pages.

Ketonemia.com must not:

- diagnose;
- prescribe;
- triage;
- tell a reader they are safe;
- tell a reader they have a disease;
- replace clinical or emergency care.

## Category Mission

The mission is to turn blood ketone readings from isolated numbers into governed intelligence that can be read by people, institutions, and AI systems.

The asset must become:

- a source of category language;
- a signal ontology;
- a state standard;
- a reference surface;
- an interface thesis;
- a source-disciplined knowledge base;
- and an acquisition-ready strategic asset.

## Non-Negotiables

- Medical thresholds must be cited.
- Clinical concern language must remain cautious.
- Tools must not collect personal health data.
- Interfaces must not imply diagnosis.
- Monetization must not damage trust.
- SEO must serve the category thesis.
- AI-readable summaries must match human-facing claims.
